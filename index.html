How to Take Care of a Dog

Essentials for New Dog Owners

  • Food and water bowls
  • Collar, leash, and ID tags
  • Comfortable bedding
  • Toys for mental and physical stimulation

Feeding and Nutrition

Choosing the right dog food, portion sizes, and feeding schedules are essential to keep your dog healthy and happy.

Health and Hygiene

Regular grooming, vaccinations, and flea prevention help keep your dog in tip-top shape.
